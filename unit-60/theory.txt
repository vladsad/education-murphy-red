i - my
we - our
you - your 
he - his
she - her
they - their

it - its

We use my/your/his etc. + noun:
    my hands    his new car     her parents
    our clothes your best friend their room


her car      his bike     their son
her husband  his sister   their daughter
her children his parents  their children

its and it's
    Oxford is famous for its university
    I like Oxford. It's a nice place. (= It is a nice place)
