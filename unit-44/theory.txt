is it .. ? have you..? do they..? etc (questions 1)

positive
    you are
question
    are you

In questions, the first verb (is/are/have etc.) is before the subject:
    positive
        subject + verb
    question
        verb + subject  (Remember: the subject is after the first verb)

do ..? does ..? did ..?

present simple questions
    do      i/we/you/they      work
    does    he she it          live
past simple questionss
    did     i/they/he/she/etc  go

Sometimes do is the main verb (do you do / did he do etc.)

Why isn’t ... ? / Why don’t ... ? etc. (Why + negative)
